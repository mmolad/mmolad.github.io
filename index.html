Hi, I'm Mickey.

My name is Mickey Molad and I'm a communications professional doing work in the social media, community management, and web marketing spaces.

You've probably seen my community management and social media work with several famous companies: Blizzard Entertainment, AMD, Certain Affinity.

Work Experience

Since 2007, I've been working in the gaming and high tech space. I started as a simple customer service agent ("Game Master"), and grew to building teams and processes spanning the entire globe. I've been at it for over 15 years now, with a diverse repeotoire with a focus on user engagement, growth, and high stakes communications.

At Blizzard Entertainment, I helped launch our Global Customer Support Operations department, giving customer service agents the tools and knowledge they needed to transition the company from traditional games to games as a service (GaaS). I led a team of knowledge management experts as we enabled customers to help themselves, while also empowering our agents to help players effectively and effeciently.

At AMD, I launched several products, including a reboot of our gaming and graphics lineup in an incredibly competitive field. I managed communications and social media for go-to-market campaigns, including paid social advertising and influencer marketing. I helped rebuild our gaming brand, focusing on our AMD Radeon products, and partnerships with Microsoft Xbox, Sony PlayStation, and the Valve Steam Deck.

Most recently, at Certain Affinity, I was head of all communications, helping revamp our external and internal procedures, rebrand ourselves on social media, and rebuild our entire web presence. I served as crisis communications manager helping ensure our brand reputation as a top AAA developer remained intact despite a challenging industry landscape.

As a volunteer, I help operate several gaming fan communities, including Overwatch and Hearthstone on Reddit and Discord. I've helped grow these communities from just a few thousand members to millions worldwide, becoming by far the biggest sources of community discussion for these games.

As part of my web design work, I've also contributed to the subreddit designs of Corsair, Death Stranding, Rocket League, Black Desert Online, Lawbreakers, ArcheAge, RUST, and more. Components of my UX and design work were also directly integrated into the official Reddit Redesign.
